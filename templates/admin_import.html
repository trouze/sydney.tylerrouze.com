How it works

The uploaded sheet becomes the complete guest list. Required column: party_label. Optional: invite_code, first_name, last_name, email, is_plus_one (yes/1/x).

Rows keep their invite_code from the export — those parties are updated in place, so QR codes and links don't change. Leave invite_code blank for a new party. Any party missing from the sheet is removed.

You'll see exactly what will be added, updated, and removed before anything is saved.
